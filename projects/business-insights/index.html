Business Insights Project

Revenue Trends

Goal: Understand seasonality and revenue cycles.

  1. Identify monthly revenue patterns across the 2.5-year dataset.
  2. Highlight holiday-driven spikes in November–December.
  3. Measure January drop-off and recovery trends.
  4. Recommend Q4 inventory ramp-ups and marketing spend.
  5. Design retention-focused campaigns for post-holiday softness.

Product Performance

Goal: Evaluate category/subcategory contribution to revenue.

  1. Rank categories by total revenue (Electronics, Apparel lead).
  2. Drill down into subcategories (Mobile Accessories, Women’s Apparel).
  3. Spot underperformers like Toys and Beauty vs spend levels.
  4. Recommend reallocating marketing spend toward high ROI categories.
  5. Suggest bundling weak categories with stronger ones during campaigns.

Customer Segments

Goal: Analyze account types and regional performance.

  1. Segment customers by Premium vs Non-Premium membership.
  2. Compare average order value (Premium yields higher AOV).
  3. Review regional momentum (South & Midwest growing, West lagging).
  4. Recommend upsell campaigns for Premium memberships.
  5. Target West region with localized promotions.

Marketing Channels

Goal: Compare conversion & ROI across acquisition channels.

  1. Measure conversion rates by channel (Email highest, Paid Search volume).
  2. Compute ROI on spend — Email/Referral outperform; Display weak.
  3. Recommend shifting budget from Display → Email & Referral.
  4. Enhance Email segmentation for loyalty, lapsed, and prospect groups.
  5. Refine Paid Search keyword bidding to improve ROI efficiency.

Returns & Customer Experience

Goal: Reduce return rate and improve CX.

  1. Analyze ~6% return rate across categories.
  2. Identify high-return categories (Apparel, Electronics accessories).
  3. Recommend size/fit recommendation tools for Apparel.
  4. Improve product descriptions/photos for Electronics.
  5. Implement structured return-reason tracking for future analysis.

Growth Opportunities

Goal: Identify future levers for expansion.

  1. Spot high-growth regions (South strongest 6-month momentum).
  2. Recommend investment in regional logistics & warehouses.
  3. Leverage customer retention — 35% of sessions tied to known customers.
  4. Encourage account sign-ups with registration discounts.
  5. Develop loyalty program expansions for repeat buyers.
